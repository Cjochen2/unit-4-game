Batman Villain's Challenge

Your objective is to guess the randomnly generated number at the top of the page.

Below are 4 images each hiding a number. When you click on the image it will add the hidden number to your score counter at the bottom. By using these 4 hidden numbers you want to slowly increase your counter to be equal to the number at the top of the page with the least number of clicks.

If you successfully match the number you will increase your win score by one. If you go over though you will lose and your failure will be recorded. After each round the pictures will be reassigned new numbers and you will have to guess a new random number. Good Luck!
